FAQ

Common Questions

Is ReWear free to use? +
Yes! Listing and exchanging items on ReWear is completely free for the community. The Marketplace features discounted items from verified providers at accessible prices.
Does ReWear give verified products? +
Yes! Our team verifies every product as soon as the customer buys it online. We ensure condition accuracy, authenticity, and quality before the item is dispatched to the buyer.
What condition should items be in? +
We accept items in Like New, Good, or Fair condition. No torn, heavily stained, or unwearable items please. Be honest in your listing — the community trusts each other!
How does the Marketplace work for buyers? +
The Marketplace features verified deadstock and unsold clothing from local shops, boutiques and brands at discounted prices. All items are verified by the ReWear team before listing.
What cities is ReWear available in? +
We're currently active in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Surat, Lucknow, Raipur, and expanding fast!
